# DanceCam Demo Video Prompt

Create a 10-15 second vertical product demo video for an Android app called DanceCam.

Style: clean modern mobile-app promo, realistic phone screen mockups, fast but understandable pacing, Korean UI labels, no exaggerated sci-fi visuals, no extra branding besides DanceCam.

Scene plan:

1. 0-3s: Problem
   Show a person trying to film a dance practice alone. The phone is on a tripod, music is playing from a music app, but normal camera recording either misses the music or captures room noise. On-screen Korean text: "혼자 춤출 때, 음악까지 깔끔하게 녹화하기 어렵죠."

2. 3-6s: Solution
   Show the DanceCam app opening on an Android phone. The camera preview fills the screen. Buttons are visible: REC, camera switch, portrait/landscape mode, zoom controls. On-screen Korean text: "DanceCam은 카메라 영상과 내부 재생 음악을 함께 저장합니다."

3. 6-9s: How to use
   Show the user tapping REC, approving Android capture permission, then dancing while music continues. Show a simple status: "녹화 중". On-screen Korean text: "REC 누르고, 캡처 승인하고, 춤추면 끝."

4. 9-12s: Orientation option
   Show the user toggling between "세로" and "가로" recording modes. Brief split-screen showing vertical short-form output and horizontal practice-room output. On-screen Korean text: "세로/가로 촬영을 바로 선택."

5. 12-15s: Result
   Show the saved file appearing in Gallery under a DanceCam folder. The video plays with clean music audio and no room noise. On-screen Korean text: "갤러리에 MP4로 저장 완료."

Important details:
- The app is Android-only.
- Do not show a microphone being used.
- Do not show screen recording of the phone display as the product output; it is a camera recording app.
- Make it clear that background playback music is included in the final saved MP4.
- Keep the demo grounded and practical, like a real app walkthrough.
